About

ICT Laboratory ITENAS is a community focused on AI, cybersecurity, and other technological fields, providing a platform for students and tech enthusiasts to learn, discuss, and develop their skills. With a spirit of collaboration and innovation, We strive to create an environment that fosters curiosity, encourages technological exploration, and supports the real-world application of knowledge through hands-on projects and collaboration.

As a dynamic community, ICT Laboratory ITENAS regularly organizes workshops, seminars, training sessions, and collaborative projects. We believe that sharing knowledge and experiences accelerates skill development and fosters impactful innovations. Through partnerships with academia, industry, and other tech communities, we are committed to supporting digital talent growth and shaping a better technological future.

I am an Informatics student at Institut Teknologi Nasional Bandung, skilled in problem-solving and software development. Through various projects, I’ve honed my ability to tackle complex challenges. While design is my weakness, I’m actively improving to become more well-rounded in tech.

Outside of academics, I love outdoor adventures and exploring history and mythology. I aim to innovate with technology for a positive impact on society.
